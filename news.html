Coming Soon: Water Damage Diagnostics

We're excited to announce that Hadi's TechHut will soon offer full diagnostics and recovery options for water-damaged devices. This has been one of our most requested services, and we're in the final stages of acquiring specialized ultrasonic cleaning tools. Whether it's a spilled drink on your laptop or a dropped phone in the sink, our upcoming service aims to recover and restore functionality in as little as 48 hours.

New Software Optimization Packages

Slow performance isn't always a hardware issue. Starting next month, we're rolling out optimization services for both laptops and desktops. These include virus scans, startup cleanup, driver updates, and registry repair to help your system run like new. It’s perfect for students or remote workers who rely on smooth performance every day. This software-only service will be available for $35 as an add-on or standalone package.

Industry Watch: Right-to-Repair Laws Expanding

Recent changes in U.S. legislation are shifting in favor of right-to-repair laws, which give consumers and independent repair shops like ours greater access to parts, tools, and documentation. This means faster repairs, better pricing, and less e-waste. At Hadi’s TechHut, we support these changes and are actively preparing to expand the brands and models we can service, including Apple and Microsoft Surface devices.

Meet the Team Behind Hadi's TechHut

At the heart of Hadi's TechHut is a dedicated duo — Hadi Elhaouli and Talal Elhaouli. Together, we combine technical expertise, hands-on repair experience, and a shared passion for solving tech problems in our community.

Our Mission

We strive to make technology more accessible by helping people repair rather than replace their devices. Whether it's a cracked screen, a dead battery, or a custom PC build, we take pride in restoring function and performance with every repair. We treat every customer like family—because for us, Dearborn is home.

Our Roles

Hadi Elhaouli is the lead technician and founder of Hadi's TechHut. With a background in electronics and computer science, Hadi ensures every repair is handled with care and precision. From diagnosing motherboard issues to optimizing device performance, Hadi is hands-on with every service.

Talal Elhaouli supports operations with logistics, customer service, and tech support. Talal's commitment to excellence keeps our workflow efficient and our clients happy. He's the go-to for scheduling, diagnostics, and making sure every client gets their device back better than before.

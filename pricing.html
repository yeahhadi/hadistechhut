Repair Pricing at Hadi's TechHut

At Hadi's TechHut, we offer competitive pricing on all of our repair services, designed to provide exceptional value without compromising on quality. Whether it's a cracked screen or a faulty charging port, our prices are upfront and affordable. Most common repairs fall within the $25–$150 range depending on the device type and severity of the issue. We also offer bundle deals for multiple services on the same device.

Premium Services

Our premium services cover advanced repairs like motherboard fixes, custom PC builds, and data recovery. These services require special tools and diagnostics, and are priced accordingly. For example:

  • Motherboard Repair $150+
  • Custom PC Build Starts at $300
  • Data Recovery $200+

These repairs are done by our lead technician and include full testing and a 90-day service warranty.

Specialty Add-ons & Quick Fixes

We also offer add-on services and express repairs for clients in a hurry. These include:

  • Laptop Fan Cleaning $25
  • Battery Calibration $30
  • Console HDMI Port Replacement $100

These are ideal for minor issues or as part of a larger service package.
